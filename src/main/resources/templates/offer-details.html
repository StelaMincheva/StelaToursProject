Title

The Bucharest Christmas Market resembles German Christmas markets, bringing together 65 wooden stalls with beautiful handmade items and ornaments.
At the bazaar you can find traditional food and drinks: cheeses, smoked bacon, sausages, mulled wine, brandy. The square will be decorated with a 17-meter Christmas tree, you will find an open carousel, local folklore, traditional concerts.
For the little tourists, there will be a meeting with Santa Claus, who comes from distant Lapland.

Program:

Departure from Varna at 05:00 from "Antim I" Street, from Shumen at 06:00 from the Billa parking lot, from Razgrad at 06:45 from the Petrol gas station on the road to Ruse, from Ruse at 08:30 from the Central Railway Station parking lot.
Arrival in Bucharest around 10:30 am. Panoramic bus tour of the city with a guide: Patriarchal Cathedral, Metropolitan Hill, Unirii Square, 21st December Square, National Theater, Revolution Square, Royal Palace, Senate, University Library, Athena Temple of Music, Romagna Square, Victoria Square and the Arc de Triomphe.
After lunch - a walking tour in the Old Town. You will see the Old Princely Court of Vlad the Impaler. Here is also the largest bookstore in Romania - "Carturesti Carusel" - translated as "Carousel of Light". You will also see the old princely church - the oldest building in Bucharest, as well as the former "Louis Manuk" inn - currently a luxury hotel-restaurant.
In the late afternoon you will have free time for the Christmas market. In the evening we travel back to Bulgaria. Arrival in Ruse around 20:00, in Shumen around 21:00, in Varna around 22:00.

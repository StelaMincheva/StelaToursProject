Who are we?

StelaTours Travel Agency is a licensed tour operator and travel agent with its office in the city of Varna and holds license RK-01-2345. The company offers both own and agency products such as:

  • - Airline tickets
  • - Excursions in Europe
  • - Vacations in Europe
  • - Hotel accommodation
  • - Cruises
  • - Individual packages for personal and business trips
  • - Student camps and excursions
  • - Insurances

In our activity, we strive to provide and offer solutions fully tailored to the needs and requirements of our customers. We value correct partnership, professional and confidential service.
